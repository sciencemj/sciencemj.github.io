Where I'm coming from

I work at the intersection of business and computer science — which means I care as much about the question behind the data as the model on top of it.

Most projects start messy: raw reviews, station logs, survey dumps. They end as something a decision-maker can act on — a forecast, a segmentation, a clear chart. I like reframing the problem first (is this a supply problem, or a competition problem?) before reaching for an algorithm.
